Questions?

Q: Attire?

A: Cocktail attire requested. Please choose shoes comfortable enough for walking in grass, dancing, and joining us for the afterparty. We will also be facing the sunset, so don't forget your sunglasses.

Q: Accomodations?

A: We recommend you find lodging within the highlighted area below. There are several hotels in the area, as well as AirBnB and VRBO options. We recommend having lodging walking distance to Villa Geno, Piazza Volta, and Piazza Cavour for attending all wedding events.
At this time there are no hotel blocks, as this is not a part of Italian culture, however, if there are any changes we will let you know.

Q: Transportation?

A: Como is a walking city. It is ~15-20 min lakeside walk from Piazza Volta/city center to Villa Geno.
There will be a shuttle from the center to Villa Geno on wedding day if you do not wish to walk.
Taxis exist but may be limited. They must be booked in advanced, and can be reached by calling +39 031 271000.
If you choose to stay in a lakeside town other than Como, there are ferries to reach Como but be mindful of the schedule as they do not run late into the evening.

Q: Renting a car?

A: Be aware that all parking within the city walls requires a residential permit or payment. There are some designated areas for parking outside the city walls but spots are not guaranteed as they fill quickly.

Q: Recommendations?

A: Scroll further down for our recommendations while staying in Como.

Q: Anything else?

A: Please feel free to reach out to us with ANY questions you may have. We are so appreciative you will be joining us and want to be sure you have the best experience possible.
